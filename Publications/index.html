Time-Reversal-Symmetry Breaking Superconductivity in SnTe Nanowires

  1. "Josephson Detection of Time Reversal Symmetry Breaking Superconductivity in SnTe Nanowires", C.J. Trimble*, M.T. Wei*, N.F.Q. Yuan, S.S. Kalantre, P. Liu, J.J. Cha, L. Fu, and J.R. Williams, preprint on arXiv:1907.04199 (2019). *:These authors contribute equally. Download from arXiv
    - We observed an unusual broken time reversal symmetry in the current-voltage characteristics of a Josephson junction made with SnTe nanowires, which can be attributed to the interband scattering between two bands of SnTe in the ferroelectric phase.
